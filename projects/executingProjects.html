Projects Current Projects

  • 1) C2+ Recovery Plant

    This project is located near Mohr city in Fars province. Contract (EPCC) price is 270 million euro and includes all aspects of basic and detail engineering, procurements (128 main requisition) and construction (4 process units, 300,000 inch piping, 115 heavy equipment installation such as cold box, turbine & compressor, De-methanizer towers and turbo expander, industrial and non-industrial buildings and …) all mentioned items are contracted with a consortium of HAMPA (P, C) and HEDCO (E) who runs the first step of ethane recovery program.

  • 2) Fractianation Plant

    This project is located near Assalouye city in Boushehr province. Contract (EPCC) price is 190 million euro and includes all aspects of basic and detail engineering, procurements (142 main requisition) and construction (SK process units, 220,000 inch piping, 135 heavy equipment installation such as ethanizer, botanizer, propanizer, industrial and non-industrial buildings and …) all mentioned items are contracted with a consortium of HAMPA (P, C) and HEDCO (E) who runs the final processing step of ethane recovery program.program.

  • 3) Mohr Utility Supply Plant

    This project is located near Mohr city in Fars province (in the same area with Mohr process project) and the main function of this project is to secure air, nitrogen and kind of water for the process areas of plant. Contract has been signed with HIRBOD NIROO company about 17.5 million euro.

  • 4) Assalouye Utility Supply

    This project is located near Mohr city in Boushehr province (in south of Assalouye process project) and the main function of this project is to secure air, nitrogen, electrical power and water steam for the process areas of plant. Contract is going to be signed with Mobin petrochemical company about 20 million euro.

  • 5) C2+ Transfer Pipeline

    Transferring liquid C2+ between Mohr process and Assalouye process will be done by a 60km 18 inch’s pipeline. We are going to use the news pipeline technology in the word by hire Austrian LCS co. as our contractor.

  • 6) Power transmission Line

    This project worth about 6.5 20 million euro and located between Akhand (132 kv power station) and Mohr plant. First estimation of the power lines indicate about 27 Km of 18mv which will be done by Parsian post development co.

  • 7) C5+ storage tanks

    One of the most valuable product of Ethan recovery plan is C5+. It must be storage in safe tanks. Paydar poolad co. is responsible to develop 2 tanks units (70,000 barrels ) as an EPC contractor .Contract price is about 13 million euro and will finish until end of 2017.

  • 8) Feed & Products Pipelines

    Feed of C2+ Recovery plant in MOHR will supply from existing PARSIAN Refinery by a 1 Km pipeline and produced Methane will return to this refinery again by another a 1 Km pipeline. In ASSALUYEH, Our products C3, C4 will be transferred to export docks by four a 7 Km pipelines from fractionation plant. The C5+ will be transferred to GREEN Storage yard or export docks by a two almost 10 Km pipeline too.

    Collectively, these lines are almost 40 Km, and at now are under design by HAMPA/HEDCO co.
