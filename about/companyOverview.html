About Company overview

Who we are

Palayesh Parsian sepehr is a private oil and gas company as subsidiary of Tadbir energy foundation which runs Ethan recovery program including 7 major (2Processing projects, pipeline project, 2utility projects, Power transferring project and C5+ storage tanks project) and some small projects.

What we do

Company main responsibly is developing Ethan recovery program (however feeding gas is from Parsian gas co.) and producing C2+,C3, C4 and C5+ in two different refineries based in Mohr ( Fars province ) and Assalouye ( Boushehr province ) . These two Plants will be connected to each other by a 60Km pipeline who carries C2+ from Mohr to Assalouye.

How we do it

To be more detail, gas feed from Parsian gas co. is combined gas including ethane (C2H6) and methane (CH4). At the first step in Mohr process units these particular parts will be separate out from each other, then methane part send back to Parsian gas co. and remaining gas convert to liquid C2+ after some special process. This liquid C2+ transfer to Assalouye by 18 inches pipeline which crosses Zagros Mountain.

Process continues in Assalouye by means of preparing other gas cuts such as C3, C4 and C5+ from received C2+. All new products in first step will be storage in special double walls tanks to be ready for delivering to domestic costumers or export to foreign by a 7km pipeline from plant to condensate terminal Jetty as final stage .
