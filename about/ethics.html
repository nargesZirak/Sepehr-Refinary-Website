Palayesh Parsian Sepehr Co. Code of Ethics

We, the employees of Palayesh Parsian Sepehr Co. by seeking help from God and on the basis of exalted human values and dignity, place ourselves responsible for and committed to all Stakeholders: our customers, consumers, citizens, neighbors, employees. We also take this oath of responsibility and commitment to all our fellow organizations, suppliers, contractors and competitors. We vow to be loyal to the prerogatives of all parties in the professional activities in which we are involved and to meet our ethical commitments which are listed in this document.
